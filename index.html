Magnetic Stirrer

A magnetic stirrer is a laboratory device that uses a rotating magnetic field to spin a magnetic stir bar within a container, facilitating efficient mixing and agitation of liquids in scientific experiments and processes.

Mini Lab Centrifuge

A mini lab centrifuge is a compact laboratory instrument designed to spin small volumes of samples at high speeds, allowing for the separation of substances based on density and providing convenient and rapid sample preparation in scientific research and diagnostics.
